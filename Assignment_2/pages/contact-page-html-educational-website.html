Who are we?

We are a group of diverse students all united to improve our web development skill. Even though we all have different study backgrounds the moment we set foot into that online teams chat was a moment to never forget. We were all asking for a group until suddenly one anonymous person found his calling and connected this epic trio. Our first meeting was the most remarkable one. Hearing the song “jingle bells” being whistled two months after Christmas, by two unexpected visitors, Micheal and Maya. This extraordinary development evolved the group from a simple trio into a marvelous quintet, with lots of web development adventures to come...
